Why Isolation Forest

Why it fits

  • No labeled fraud data required
  • Scales to large claims datasets
  • No distance or density assumptions
  • Outputs a continuous risk score

How It Works

  • Builds many random decision trees
  • Each split: random feature, random threshold
  • Anomalies get isolated in fewer splits
  • Shorter path length → higher risk score
Unsupervised, scalable, and needs no fraud labels

Why Groq Llama

Why choose it

  • Free tier with very fast inference
  • LPU hardware → near real-time output
  • Simple REST API, easy to call from Power Query
  • Strong reasoning for claim-level narratives

How It Works

  • Top risk-scored claims sent as a prompt
  • Model reasons over claim-level features
  • Returns a plain-language explanation
  • Rendered as AI Insights in the dashboard
